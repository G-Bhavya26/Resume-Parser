3. Layer 5: Evidence-Based Scoring

Total Score = (Proj * 0.25) + (Skill * 0.20) + (Trust * 0.15) + (Intern * 0.15) + (Edu * 0.15) + (Sem * 0.10)
  • Z-Score: Normalizes rank relative to the batch mean.
  • Hidden Text: Immediate Disqualification (Detection required in Layer 2/5).
  • Override: HR can adjust scores, but must log an immutable reason.
